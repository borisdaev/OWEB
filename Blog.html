Macedonia Beats Georgia To Qualify For First-Ever Major Soccer Tournament

Nov 13, 2020

Macedonia will go to a major soccer tournament for the first time in its history after team captain Goran Pandev's goal gave the Balkan nation a 1-0 win away over Georgia in their Euro 2020 qualifying playoff final on November 12. Pandev scored in the 56th minute in Tbilisi, allowing the country to secure their place in the finals of the postponed European Championship, which is now set to go ahead in June and July next year.

VARDAR CLAIM SECOND CHAMPIONS LEAGUE TITLE

June 3, 2019

The first weekend of June saw a celebration worthy of what was the 10th edition of the EHF FINAL4 in Cologne, Germany, where the EHF Champions League season culminated in a thrilling three-day event featuring an action-packed opening day including a live concert and meet-and-greet with the players, the semi-finals, and the finals. The most coveted trophy in men’s European club handball was won by Macedonian team HC Vardar, who wrote an epic story of underdogs triumphing thanks to sheer will and fighting spirit.
